Biography

Xiaochun Li received her B.S. in electronic engineering and the M.S. degree in telecommunication from Tianjin University, China, in 1999 and 2002, respectively, and the Ph. D. degree in electronic science and technology from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, in 2007. In 2002 she joined the Department of Electronic Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and became an associate professor in 2009. From 2008 to 2009, she was a Visiting Scholar in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology.

Dr. Li has published more than 30 articles in international journals and conferences and has two computer software copyrights. She won National Excellent Ph. D Dissertation nomination award of China and Shanghai Excellent Ph.D. Dissertation Award in 2011, Morning Star Young Scholars Award of Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2010, Second–class National Technology Invention Award of China in 2008, and First-class Science and Technology Research Award of Shanghai in 2005. Her research interests include High-speed circuit modeling, simulation and optimization, Signal Integrity and Power Integrity, and CAD techniques of VLSI.
